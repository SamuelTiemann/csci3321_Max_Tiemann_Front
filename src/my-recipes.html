Tex Mex Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of rice
  • 10 oz green chiles
  • 1 lb grond beef, browned(8-10 minutes)
  • 4 cups tortilla chips, crushed
  • 15 oz pinto beans
  • 1 lb Velveeta cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Prepare rice according to instructions; set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine cooked rice, green chiles, and ground beef; set aside.
  4. Cover 13x9x2 inch baking dish bottom with tortilla chips. Layer half of the cheeseover the tortilla chips. top with beans and rice-chile-beef mixture. Top with remaining cheese.
  5. Bake, uncovered for 15 minutesor until cheese is melted.

Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Spaghetti noodles
  • 1 lb italian sausage, browned
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce

Directions

  1. Cook noodles according to instructions; set aside
  2. Mix sausage and pasta sauce in large pan; bring to boil, then remove from heat.
  3. Serve sausage sauce over cooked pasta.

Tortilla Pizzas

Ingredients

  • 1 package Tortillas
  • 1 jar Pizza Sauce
  • 1 lb Shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • Pizza Toppings (if desired)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F.
  2. Place tortilla(s) on baking sheet. Lather tortillas thickly with pizza sauce.
  3. Cover tortillas with cheese, add pizza toppings and extra cheese if desired.
  4. Broil tortillas for 3 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
